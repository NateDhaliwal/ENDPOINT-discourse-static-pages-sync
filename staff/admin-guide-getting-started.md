<p><em>Welcome to your new community, and thank you for choosing Discourse!</em></p>
<h2><a name="e_mail-test-your-email-configuration-1" class="anchor" href="#e_mail-test-your-email-configuration-1"></a><img src="/images/emoji/twitter/e_mail.png?v=14" title=":e_mail:" class="emoji" alt=":e_mail:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> Test your email configuration</h2>
<p>Email is required for new account signups and notifications.</p>
<p>→ Send a <strong><a href="http://localhost:30007D/admin/email/server-settings" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"><kbd>test email</kbd></a></strong>.</p>
<p>→ Email didn’t arrive? Read our guide on <a href="https://github.com/discourse/discourse/blob/main/docs/INSTALL-email.md">email providers</a>.</p>
<h2><a name="handshake-invite-your-team-2" class="anchor" href="#handshake-invite-your-team-2"></a><img src="/images/emoji/twitter/handshake.png?v=14" title=":handshake:" class="emoji" alt=":handshake:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> Invite your team</h2>
<p>Collaborating with others?</p>
<p>→ <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/new-invite"><kbd>Send invites</kbd></a></strong> to your team members.</p>
<h2><a name="speech_balloon-start-some-conversations-3" class="anchor" href="#speech_balloon-start-some-conversations-3"></a><img src="/images/emoji/twitter/speech_balloon.png?v=14" title=":speech_balloon:" class="emoji" alt=":speech_balloon:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> Start some conversations</h2>
<p>Give your early members a place to start interacting.</p>
<p>→ Add some topics with conversation starters, frequently asked questions, and other content.</p>
<p>→ Discuss ideas and plans for your community with your team.</p>
<h2><a name="artist_palette-make-it-your-own-4" class="anchor" href="#artist_palette-make-it-your-own-4"></a><img src="/images/emoji/twitter/artist_palette.png?v=14" title=":artist_palette:" class="emoji" alt=":artist_palette:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> Make it your own</h2>
<p>Help your new members feel right at home when they first arrive.</p>
<p>→ Personalize your <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/t/-/5/"><kbd>welcome topic</kbd></a></strong> and fill out your <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/about"><kbd>about page</kbd></a></strong>.</p>
<p>→ Customize your <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/admin/config/logo"><kbd>site appearance</kbd></a></strong>.</p>
<p>→ Set up different <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/admin/config/login-and-authentication"><kbd>login methods</kbd></a></strong> for your members.</p>
<h2><a name="waving_hand-invite-your-first-members-5" class="anchor" href="#waving_hand-invite-your-first-members-5"></a><img src="/images/emoji/twitter/waving_hand.png?v=14" title=":waving_hand:" class="emoji" alt=":waving_hand:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20">  Invite your first members</h2>
<p>Welcome a few people in - the more, the merrier!</p>
<p>→ Send out <strong><a href="http://localhost:3000/new-invite"><kbd>invites</kbd></a></strong> or send people the link to your community.</p>
